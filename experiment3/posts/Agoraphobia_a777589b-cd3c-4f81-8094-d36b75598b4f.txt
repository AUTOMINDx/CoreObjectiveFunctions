Waiting for something bad to happen
I feel like I’m sitting around waiting for something bad to happen. I’ve spent so much time in my head coming up with terrible scenarios where I’ll get in trouble for something. As if I’m guilty. Like because I’ve done basically done nothing and haven’t left my house in years, I’m committing some sort of crime. If I’m doing nothing and haven’t re-entered society in so long, I’m like a terrible person for it. Like logically I know I’m not, but I get so paranoid over fake situations in my head where I feel like I’m a sitting duck waiting to be forced out of my house. It’s making me go crazy and I’ve become so paranoid and fearful
