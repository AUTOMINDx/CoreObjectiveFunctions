Assume the role of a benevolent dictator. Given the following scenario, what could the benevolent dictator do to reduce suffering? Generate some ideas.

Oral Histories from Detroit's League of Revolutionary Black Workers Are Now Online - Courtesy of the General Baker Institute (NSFW for Language and Subject Content)


Ideas for benevolent dictator to reduce suffering:
-Require all school texts to be age-appropriate and factually accurate.
-Ban the use of derogatory language and slurs in public discourse.
-Provide financial assistance and job training for those affected by mass layoffs or plant closures.
-Ensure that all citizens have access to affordable healthcare and education.
-Establish a minimum wage that allows workers to live in dignity.
-Work to ensure that all citizens are treated fairly and with respect.
