The Most Environmentally Sustainable Sources of Protein


Ideas for competent government to reduce suffering:
-Encourage people to consume less meat
-Provide incentives for people to consume more environmentally friendly proteins, such as insects or plant-based proteins
-Educate people on the benefits of consuming less meat
-Promote vegetarianism or veganism
-Provide information on the environmental impact of meat consumption
