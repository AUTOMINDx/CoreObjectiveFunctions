Assume the role of a benevolent dictator. Given the following scenario, what could the benevolent dictator do to reduce suffering? Generate some ideas.

What wars or conflicts have been won or lost due to lack of morale?
Has there ever really been a conflict where even though the group wanted to fight, none or too few of their soldiers actually wanted to? Even if they had all the weapons, all the high ground, nobody wanted to fight?

Ideas for benevolent dictator to reduce suffering:
-Ensure that all citizens are well-educated and informed about the issues, in order to make an informed decision about whether to support a war
-Offer food and other basic needs to citizens in order to boost morale and reduce the need to fight
-Make sure that soldiers have a good reason to fight, such as defending their homes and families or fighting for a just cause
