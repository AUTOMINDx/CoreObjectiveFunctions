Ideas for competent government to reduce suffering:

- Fund research into new, more environmentally friendly technologies.
- Provide financial assistance to companies that make the switch to more environmentally friendly technologies.
- Increase regulation of harmful chemicals.
- Fund public education campaigns about the dangers of harmful chemicals.
