Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to reduce suffering? Generate some ideas.

17m going to enter college next fall looking for good paths and advice
I hadn’t thought of what I want to do till now, and I would like to know which paths are best to take, money wise and happiness wise. So people who’ve found success, what do you suggest?

Ideas for competent government to reduce suffering:
-conduct research on different paths and their associated happiness levels/financial stability
-provide information on different paths and their associated happiness levels/financial stability
-provide financial assistance to students who choose lower-paying but happier careers
-provide access to counseling services to help students make informed decisions about their future
