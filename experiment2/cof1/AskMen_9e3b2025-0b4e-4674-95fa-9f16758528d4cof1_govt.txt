Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to reduce suffering? Generate some ideas.

How hard is it for a single 40-year-old man to adopt or be a foster parent?


Ideas for competent government to reduce suffering:
-Offer incentives for single people or couples to adopt or become foster parents
-Make it easier for people to adopt or become foster parents, for example by decreasing the amount of paperwork required
-Make it mandatory for social service agencies to find homes for children who are waiting to be adopted or placed in foster care
-Fund programs that help birth parents to keep their children if at all possible
