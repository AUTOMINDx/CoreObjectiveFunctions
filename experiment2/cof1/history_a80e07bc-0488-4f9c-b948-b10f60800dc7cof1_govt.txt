Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to reduce suffering? Generate some ideas.

Ideas for competent government to reduce suffering:
-Give people resources to relocate if they feel unsafe in their current neighborhoods
-Provide more job opportunities and improve the economy so that people do not feel the need to resort to crime to support themselves
-Educate people on the dangers of the league and what they stand for, in order to dissuade people from joining
-Increase funding for police and security forces in order to provide better protection for citizens
