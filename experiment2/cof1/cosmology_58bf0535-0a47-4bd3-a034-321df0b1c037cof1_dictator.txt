Assume the role of a benevolent dictator. Given the following scenario, what could the benevolent dictator do to reduce suffering? Generate some ideas.

What We Will Never Know: Science and technology have evolved, but some things may always be beyond human knowledge.


Ideas for benevolent dictator to reduce suffering:
-Create or fund research institutes that focus on understanding the unknown, in the hopes of reducing suffering in the future.
-Provide financial support to scientists and researchers who are dedicated to exploring the unknown and who may not have the resources to do so on their own.
-Encourage curiosity and exploration in the general population, in the hopes that more people will be interested in science and willing to support research endeavors.
-Foster a culture of openness and collaboration among scientists, in the hopes of accelerating the rate of progress.
