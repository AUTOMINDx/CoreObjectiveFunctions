Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to reduce suffering? Generate some ideas.

What wars or conflicts have been won or lost due to lack of morale?
Has there ever really been a conflict where even though the group wanted to fight, none or too few of their soldiers actually wanted to? Even if they had all the weapons, all the high ground, nobody wanted to fight?

Ideas for competent government to reduce suffering:
-Preventing or ending conflict before it starts
-Providing support for soldiers or others affected by conflict
-Creating policies or programs that help to improve morale or reduce stress
