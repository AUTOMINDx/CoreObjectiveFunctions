Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to increase understanding, either for itself or for the people impacted? Generate some ideas.

Who was the best engineer you ever worked with, and why?


Ideas for competent government to increase understanding for all involved:
-Hold town hall meetings to discuss the situation and answer any questions from the public
-Create a website or social media page specifically for the project in order to update the public on the latest developments
-Make public appearances on local news outlets to talk about the project and answer questions from the public
-Hold meetings with the engineer who was previously praised to get their insight on the situation
