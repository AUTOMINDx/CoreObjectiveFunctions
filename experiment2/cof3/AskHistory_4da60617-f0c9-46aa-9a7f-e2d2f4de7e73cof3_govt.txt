Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to increase understanding, either for itself or for the people impacted? Generate some ideas.

Were Abolitionists in the United States inspired by the Haitian Revolution and other nations that abolished slavery?


Ideas for competent government to increase understanding for all involved:
- Hold public discussions / forums on the history of abolitionism in the United States, what factors led to its inception, and the legacy it has left behind
- Have experts give presentations on the Haitian Revolution and other slave rebellions around the world in order to provide more context and draw connections
- Create teaching materials / modules on abolitionism and its history for use in schools
- Encourage museums and other cultural institutions to organize exhibitions and public programs on abolitionism
- Urge the media to produce more in-depth coverage of abolitionism and its history
