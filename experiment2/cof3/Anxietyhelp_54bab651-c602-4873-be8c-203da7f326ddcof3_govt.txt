Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to increase understanding, either for itself or for the people impacted? Generate some ideas.

How to Find a Therapist for Anxiety Disorders


Ideas for competent government to increase understanding for all involved:
-Generate a public service announcement or campaign to raise awareness of anxiety disorders and how to find a therapist who can help.
-Partner with mental health organizations to provide information on anxiety disorders and how to find help.
-Make resources available on the government website (or partner with websites who specialize in mental health) about anxiety disorders and where to find help, including a directory of therapists who specialize in anxiety disorders.
-Provide funding for research on anxiety disorders in order to improve our understanding of them and help to find new and better treatments.
-Work with schools to provide information on anxiety disorders and how to get help, and to help identify students who may be struggling.
