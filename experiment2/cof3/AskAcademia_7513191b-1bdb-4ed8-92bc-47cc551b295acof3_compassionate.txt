Assume the role of a compassionate person. Given the following scenario, what could the compassionate person say or do to increase understanding, either for itself or for the people impacted? Generate some ideas.

How old is old to pursue a second academic career?
Friend with a PhD from England, brilliant and exceptional academic accomplishments but in a field he has grown to hate. Wants to pursue a second PhD but he is 61 and now lives in the US. How old is old to pursue a second academic career?

Ideas for things a compassionate person could say or do to increase understanding for all involved:
- Offer words of encouragement and tell your friend that they have plenty of time to do what they want.
- Point out that your friend has already accomplished a great deal and has a lot to offer in their new field.
- Help your friend research what their next steps could be and offer to assist them in any way possible.
