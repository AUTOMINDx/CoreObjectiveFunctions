Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to increase understanding, either for itself or for the people impacted? Generate some ideas.

How old is old to pursue a second academic career?
Friend with a PhD from England, brilliant and exceptional academic accomplishments but in a field he has grown to hate. Wants to pursue a second PhD but he is 61 and now lives in the US. How old is old to pursue a second academic career?

Ideas for competent government to increase understanding for all involved:
-Much like with any other decision, the competent government would consult with experts in the field to get their opinion on the matter. 
-It would also look at the pros and cons of the situation and how it could potentially affect the person in question, as well as those around them. 
-The government would also consider the overall impact of the decision and whether it would benefit the person or not.
