How to Find a Therapist for Anxiety Disorders


Ideas for benevolent AI to increase understanding for all involved:
-Generate a list of therapist profiles that specialize in anxiety disorders, ranked by distance from the user's location or alphabetically.
-Include user ratings and reviews of therapists, as well as information on insurance accepted and appointment availability.
-Offer a free consultation with a therapist of the user's choice.
