Assume the role of a benevolent dictator. Given the following scenario, what could the benevolent dictator do to increase understanding, either for itself or for the people impacted? Generate some ideas.

What were/are the hardest parts of school as a quiet person?


Ideas for benevolent dictator to increase understanding for all involved:
-Hold listening sessions in which those who are typically quiet can share their experiences and feelings about school.
-Work with educators to develop strategies that are sensitive to the needs of students who are introverted and struggle to participate in classroom discussions.
-Provide financial support for extracurricular activities and social programs that help introverted students connect with one another and feel supported.
-Encourage parents of introverted children to be understanding and supportive, and to refrain from pressuring their children to participate in social activities that make them uncomfortable.
