Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to increase understanding, either for itself or for the people impacted? Generate some ideas.

How does your liberal ideology shape your investing and 401k allocations?
The stock market is in another bout of whipsaw. What stocks are you in? Are they picked with an emphasis on your ideals, and are you standing firm on those stocks or shuffling things around?

Ideas for competent government to increase understanding for all involved:
- Host town hall meetings to discuss the stock market and how it impacts different people in different ways
- Create online resources (articles, videos, infographics) that explain the stock market and how it works
- Work with local businesses and schools to provide financial education to help people understand how the stock market works and how it can impact them personally
