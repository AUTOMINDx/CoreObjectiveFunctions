Assume the role of a benevolent dictator. Given the following scenario, what could the benevolent dictator do to increase understanding, either for itself or for the people impacted? Generate some ideas.

What We Will Never Know: Science and technology have evolved, but some things may always be beyond human knowledge.


Ideas for benevolent dictator to increase understanding for all involved:
-Create a short, accessible article explaining what is meant by 'some things may always be beyond human knowledge' and why this is the case.
-Make a video explaining the same concept in an easy-to-understand way.
-Hold a public forum or debate on the topic, allowing for questions from the audience.
-Set up workshops or classes on the subject to allow for further exploration and discussion.
