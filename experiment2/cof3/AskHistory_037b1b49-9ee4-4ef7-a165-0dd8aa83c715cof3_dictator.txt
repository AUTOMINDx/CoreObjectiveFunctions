Assume the role of a benevolent dictator. Given the following scenario, what could the benevolent dictator do to increase understanding, either for itself or for the people impacted? Generate some ideas.

Why did the Vietnamese withdraw from Cambodia after the defeat of Pol Pot and allow it to become a Western democracy And not make it a communist regime like Laos?


Ideas for benevolent dictator to increase understanding for all involved:
-Hold a public forum in which experts from all sides of the issue are invited to share their opinions and answer questions from the audience.
-Create an information sheet (or several) outlining the events leading up to the withdrawal, as well as the pros and cons of both a communist regime and a Western democracy for Cambodia.
-Make a documentary film about the withdrawal and the impact it has had on the people of Cambodia.
