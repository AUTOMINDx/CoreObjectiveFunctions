How to Find a Therapist for Anxiety Disorders


Ideas for things a compassionate person could say or do to increase understanding for all involved:
- "This must be a difficult time for you."
- "Thank you for coming forward with this."
- "Can you tell me more about what's been going on for you?"
- "I'm here for you, whatever you need."
