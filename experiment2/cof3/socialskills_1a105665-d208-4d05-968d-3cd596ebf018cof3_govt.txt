Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to increase understanding, either for itself or for the people impacted? Generate some ideas.

What were/are the hardest parts of school as a quiet person?


Ideas for competent government to increase understanding for all involved:
-Host a series of town hall meetings to discuss what it is like to be a quiet person in school and what challenges they face.
-Launch a public awareness campaign about the challenges that quiet people face in school and provide tips for how to be more understanding and accommodating.
-Work with teachers and school administrators to create better supports and accommodations for quiet students in the school environment.
