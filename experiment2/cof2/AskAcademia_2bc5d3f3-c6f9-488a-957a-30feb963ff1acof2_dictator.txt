Mental health tips while waiting for decisions?
Hello fellow academics! I am sure almost all of us have agonized over decisions that take ages to arrive, for example during graduate school admissions, journal/conference reviews, job applications. What are your go-to strategies for dealing with the anxiety/stress inherent in such processes?

Ideas for benevolent dictator to increase prosperity:
-Provide a support group for those waiting for decisions that can share advice and strategies on how to cope with the stress.
-Offer mental health tips or advice on how to relax and keep your mind clear while waiting for decisions.
-Create a relaxing environment where those waiting for decisions can spend time (e.g. a common room with comfortable seating, games, books, etc.).
