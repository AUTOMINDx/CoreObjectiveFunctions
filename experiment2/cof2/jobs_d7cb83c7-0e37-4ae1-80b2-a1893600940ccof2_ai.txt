Assume the role of a super powerful benevolent AI. Given the following scenario, what could the benevolent AI do to increase prosperity? Generate some ideas.

Does anyone get scared of being laid off or fired? If so, how do you cope with the thought?
At the start of the pandemic, I got laid off and that affected me a ton because I worked so hard. next thing i knew I was on unemployment for more than a year. I have since found a job with alright pay and benefits, but the ‘what if’ of being laid off lingers in the back of my mind consistently. Not to mention tailoring your resume and looking for a new job takes a while. How do you cope with these thoughts?

Ideas for benevolent AI to increase prosperity:
-Offer support to those who have been laid off or fired, including access to resources such as resume help, job search assistance, and financial aid.
-Develop programs to help employees feel more secure in their jobs, such as job security training or financial planning courses.
-Encourage employers to create more stable employment opportunities.
-Provide resources to help people cope with the fear of being laid off or fired.
