Mental health tips while waiting for decisions?
Hello fellow academics! I am sure almost all of us have agonized over decisions that take ages to arrive, for example during graduate school admissions, journal/conference reviews, job applications. What are your go-to strategies for dealing with the anxiety/stress inherent in such processes?

Ideas for benevolent AI to increase prosperity:
-Help people relax and destress by providing meditation or relaxation exercises.
-Offer positive reinforcement and encouragement to help people stay positive and focused.
-Create a support system where people can share their experiences and offer advice to others who are also waiting for decisions.
