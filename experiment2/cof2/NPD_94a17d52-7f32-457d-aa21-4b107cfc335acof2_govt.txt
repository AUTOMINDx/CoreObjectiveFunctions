Ideas for competent government to increase prosperity:
-Work towards improving infrastructure, such as increasing number of paved roads, developing a more effective public transportation system, and improving access to clean water and sanitation
-Encourage foreign investment and maintain a business-friendly environment
-Establish special economic zones to incentivize businesses to set up shop in your country
-Focus on education and training, so that your citizens are equipped with the skills needed to compete in the global economy
-Promote entrepreneurship and innovation by creating an environment conducive to risk-taking and creativity
-Reduce bureaucracy and regulations that impede business growth
