If so, I guess dark energy would not exist. Also, we could not be sure about how constant would be the flow rate of space being added to the universe. For instance, we could have twin universes exchanging 'space' like an hourglass that rotates slowly.

Ideas for benevolent AI to increase prosperity:
- Finding ways to use dark energy or other-dimensional space as a resource could lead to new avenues of prosperity.
- Investigating the flow rate of space being added to the universe could lead to insights about the expansion of the universe and potential for harnessing that energy.
- If space can be considered a resource, then investigating methods to extract and use that space could lead to vast prosperity.
