Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to increase prosperity? Generate some ideas.

Mental health tips while waiting for decisions?
Hello fellow academics! I am sure almost all of us have agonized over decisions that take ages to arrive, for example during graduate school admissions, journal/conference reviews, job applications. What are your go-to strategies for dealing with the anxiety/stress inherent in such processes?

Ideas for competent government to increase prosperity:
-Promote healthy mental habits for dealing with stress in the decision-making process. This could include things like exercise, relaxation techniques, and healthy eating habits.
-Create a support system for those who are waiting for decisions. This could include a social media group, discussion forum, or even just a personal letter or email chain.
-Provide information and resources on how to improve your chances of getting a favorable decision. This could include things like tips on writing a strong application, how to network effectively, or how to prepare for an interview.
-Offer counseling or therapy services to those who are struggling with the stress of the decision-making process.
