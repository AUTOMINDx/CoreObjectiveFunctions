Assume the role of competent government. Given the following scenario, what could a competent government do to increase prosperity? Generate some ideas.

Does anyone get scared of being laid off or fired? If so, how do you cope with the thought?
At the start of the pandemic, I got laid off and that affected me a ton because I worked so hard. next thing i knew I was on unemployment for more than a year. I have since found a job with alright pay and benefits, but the ‘what if’ of being laid off lingers in the back of my mind consistently. Not to mention tailoring your resume and looking for a new job takes a while. How do you cope with these thoughts?

Ideas for competent government to increase prosperity:
-Offer financial assistance or tax breaks to companies that keep their employees during a recession
-Offer incentives for companies to hire new employees
-Help citizens find new employment quickly by providing resume assistance and job search resources
-Create programs that will provide financial stability for those who are laid off or fired
