Here's why you should always negotiate
People often miss the chance to negotiate out of fear, but doing it can actually help you.

At best, you’ll get what you want.

And at worst, they’ll respect you for negotiating.

The risk of getting rejected outright is much lower than people think if you’re thoughtful about how you push back.
