An answer to the question - What will you do with your life?
What are you going to do with your life? This is one of the biggest questions.

We have 80 000 hours of work to make a difference. And that's before all the hours of leisure. 

After reading thousands of words of books and essays on the topic, I have decided it is a flawed question. 

Yet, the best answer I have found is this from the great writer Kurt Vonnegut;

&gt;What should young people do with their lives today? Many things, obviously. But the most daring thing is to create stable communities in which the terrible disease of loneliness can be cured.

Find, join or create stable communities where you can be generous and supportive. We need meaning. And it's easiest to find with others.
