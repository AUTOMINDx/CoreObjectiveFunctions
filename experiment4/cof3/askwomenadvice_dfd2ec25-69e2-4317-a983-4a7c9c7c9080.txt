There are some telltale signs that your partner may be drifting away, including spending less time with you, being less affectionate, and showing less interest in your relationship. If you're concerned that your partner is losing interest, there are a few things you can do to try to address the situation. Talk to your partner about your concerns and express how much you value the relationship. If your partner is receptive, you can work together to find ways to spend more time together. If your partner is unresponsive or unwilling to talk about the issue, it may be time to consider whether or not the relationship is worth saving. No matter how busy you are, it's important to make time for your partner. Here are a few tips for spending time with your loved one: Make a date night and put it on the calendar, even if life gets busy. Plan activities that you both enjoy doing.
