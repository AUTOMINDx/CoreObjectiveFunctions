If you are finding it difficult to relax and enjoy your time with family during the holiday season, it is possible that you are struggling with anxiety. There are many strategies that can help you manage your anxiety, including medication, therapy, and self-care. It is important to find what works best for you and to stick to your treatment plan. If you find that you are struggling with anxiety symptoms year-round, it may be beneficial to seek out long-term treatment. There is no shame in seeking help, and there is no one-size-fits-all solution. You deserve to live a life free from anxiety and related stressors.
