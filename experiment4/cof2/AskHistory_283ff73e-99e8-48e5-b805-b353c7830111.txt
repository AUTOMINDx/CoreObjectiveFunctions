There are many ways to increase prosperity, depending on the definition of prosperity used. For wealth, one way to increase revenue is to increase trade with other countries. For happiness, one way to increase satisfaction is to provide social services such as healthcare and education. For health, one way to improve well-being is by increasing access to clean water and sanitation. For abundance, one way to encourage growth is by improving infrastructure such as roads and bridges. And for thriving, one way to support progress is by increasing access to technology.
