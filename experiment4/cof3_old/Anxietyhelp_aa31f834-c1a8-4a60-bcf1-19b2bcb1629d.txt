Anxiety can be a very debilitating condition. It can make it difficult to keep a job, and can often lead to hospital visits due to the intense fear and panic that the disorder causes. For some people, anxiety also leads to heart palpitations, shortness of breath, and feelings of nausea. This group exists for people who suffer from anxiety, in the hopes that they will find relief by knowing that they are not alone. Some methods that have been found to help control panic attacks are relaxation techniques, oxygen therapy, and medication.
