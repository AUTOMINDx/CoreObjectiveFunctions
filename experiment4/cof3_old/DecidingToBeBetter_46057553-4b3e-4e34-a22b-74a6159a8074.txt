It sounds like you want to do better, and that's great! A lot of self-improvement can be done by reading books. Some recommendations for books that can help you with relationships, developing a personality, and not letting things get you down easily are The 5 Love Languages by Gary Chapman, The Power of Intention by Wayne Dyer, and The Four Agreements by Don Miguel Ruiz. All of these books offer great advice and wisdom that can help you on your journey to becoming a better version of yourself. Remember to stay positive and work hard; you can do it!
